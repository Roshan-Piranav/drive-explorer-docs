# Launching Drive Explorer Pro

Once you’ve installed the _Drive Explorer Pro_ extension, follow these steps to launch and start using it in Google Sheets.

## 1. Open Google Sheets

Open any existing Google Sheet or create a new one at [sheets.google.com](https://sheets.google.com).

## 2. Access the Extension

1. Click on the **Extensions** menu in the top toolbar.  
2. From the dropdown, select **Drive Explorer Pro → Open Drive Explorer**.

## 3. Start Exploring

Once launched, the add-on panel will appear on the right side of your screen. You can now:

- Browse your Google Drive files and folders  
- Select files to insert into the sheet  
- Copy folder structures  
- Export metadata for selected files
